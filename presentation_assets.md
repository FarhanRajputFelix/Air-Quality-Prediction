I am proud to share my latest machine learning project—a deep dive into the **2025 World Air Quality Report** data using ensemble learning. Our planetary health is at a crossroads, and data science is the key to understanding regional environmental risks.

📉 **The Disparity:** While **French Polynesia** and **Iceland** enjoy pristine air (PM2.5 < 5µg/m³), regions in **South Asia** like Pakistan and North India are battling concentrations 13x higher than WHO safety limits.
